About me

Hey, I'm Yash — most people just call me Yashy. By day I'm a software engineer at HCLTech and a student at BITS Pilani; by night I'm the guy who gets weirdly attached to side-projects that have no business existing.

The running joke among people who've seen my GitHub is that I treat "free tier" as a personal challenge. My favourite trick is what I call the GitHub Actions daemon pattern — instead of paying for a server, I self-trigger workflows that run for ~5h50m, exit right before the runner's hard timeout, and kick off the next one. It's a genuinely free, always-on service, and I've used it to keep a small fleet of Discord widgets alive in real time — synced to Spotify, Last.fm, AniList, my Genshin profile, even live rocket launches. None of it needed to be built. I built it anyway.

Outside of code I draw — mostly digital fanart in Ibispaint X, usually late at night with something loud playing. I'm the kind of person who has strong, slightly unhinged opinions about Iron Man, will not shut up about the Nissan GT-R, and considers "Sunflower" by Post Malone a personality trait at this point. If you scroll up to the Arts section, that's not a portfolio flex — that's genuinely just what I do to unwind.

I'm not trying to sound impressive here. I like building things that shouldn't work, I like drawing things nobody asked for, and I like it when both of those things are a little too online. If any of that sounds like your kind of weird, scroll down and say hi.
